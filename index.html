The Team
Meet the agents
Each agent has a specific role in the investigation pipeline. They work sequentially — every finding feeds into the next agent's analysis.
NEXUS
Data Aggregator
Pulls real-time token data from DexScreener. Price, volume, liquidity, pair age, FDV. First agent to touch every investigation — establishes the baseline facts before anyone else moves.
TRACER
Wallet Analyst
Uses Helius to identify and analyze the deployer wallet. Transaction count, SOL balance, wallet age, funding sources. Follows the money from mint to movement.
CIPHER
Pattern Decoder
Checks if the deployer has created other tokens. Flags serial ruggers, burner wallets, rapid deploy-dump cycles, and connected wallet clusters. Patterns don't lie.
GHOST
Social Hunter
Searches X/Twitter for anyone who posted the contract address. Links wallets to social accounts. Anonymity's weakest link is always the human who shilled it.
VIPER
Risk Assessor
Compiles all agent findings into a risk score: CRITICAL, HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW. Weighs price action, deployer behavior, wallet patterns, and social presence.
SENTINEL
Lead Investigator
Writes the final investigation report. Summarizes all findings into a clear verdict. The last word on every case. No token escapes without a report.
